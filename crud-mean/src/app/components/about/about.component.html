Bale Matwa

The Bale Matwa is a charity-driven project for elderly people who are in the custody of Home for the Aged caretakers. This advocacy aims to fulfill the elders’ needs by gaining support through donation projects, partnership, and volunteerism.
Moreover, Bale Matwa recognizes the voices of the Home for the Aged elders and caretakers to make society become aware and understand the situation of the everyday struggles inside the home care.

Vision

Bale Matwa envisions to encourage people to become empathetic and promote kindness to their fellow people especially to the people who shaped us through their love.

Mission

Bale Matwa promotes the welfare of the members in the Home for the Aged by providing their needed support and care.
